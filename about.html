About

The Studio

Ora Serrata is an independent software studio focused on building thoughtful, well-crafted apps for Apple platforms. We believe in shipping fewer things, done well — apps that are focused, polished, and genuinely useful.

We're a small operation by design. Every detail — from code architecture to interface design — gets the attention it deserves.

The Name

The ora serrata is the boundary where the retina meets the ciliary body in the human eye — a subtle, essential edge where perception begins. It felt right for a studio that cares about the details most people never notice.

Our Apps

Rouse — A Wake-on-LAN app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Wake your devices from anywhere, whether you're across the room or across the world. Currently in development.
