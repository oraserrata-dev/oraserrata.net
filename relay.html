How it works

The relay sits between the Rouse app and your sleeping devices.

1

Install the relay

Download and run the installer on any always-on device on your network. Choose a strong password when prompted.

2

Connect via VPN

Set up Tailscale, WireGuard, or OpenVPN so you can reach your home network when you're away.

3

Configure Rouse

Open Rouse, go to Relay Setup, enter your relay's IP address and the password you set. Tap Test Connection to verify.

Requirements

Always-on device

The relay needs to run on a device that stays powered on and connected to the same network as the devices you want to wake.

VPN for remote access

To wake devices when you're away from home, you need a VPN that connects you to your home network. Tailscale is the easiest option.

Rouse app

Available on the App Store for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. The relay works with Rouse v1.0 and later.
