Rouse

Wake your devices from anywhere.

Rouse is a Wake-on-LAN app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac that lets you power on your computers remotely — whether you're across the room or across the world.

  • ◉
    Local & Remote Wake Send magic packets on your LAN, or wake devices over the internet through a lightweight relay.
  • ◉
    iCloud Sync Your device list stays in sync across all your Apple devices, automatically.
  • ◉
    Widgets & Shortcuts Wake a device from your Home Screen, Lock Screen, or with Siri — no app launch required.
  • ◉
    Built for Apple Native SwiftUI on every platform. Designed to feel right at home on iOS, iPadOS, and macOS.
