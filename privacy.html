Privacy Policy

Effective: March 13, 2026

Ora Serrata LLC ("we," "us," or "our") builds apps that respect your privacy. This policy describes how our apps handle your information.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed to collect as little data as possible. Here's what our apps may use:

  • Device configuration data you enter (such as device names, MAC addresses, and IP addresses) — stored locally on your device and synced via your personal iCloud account
  • Network information necessary to send Wake-on-LAN packets

Network & Location

Rouse requests access to your WiFi network name to organize your devices by location. This information is used locally on your device and is never transmitted or stored externally. Your precise geographic location is never accessed.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Precise geographic location data

iCloud

If you use iCloud, your device configurations may be synced across your Apple devices using Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage. This data is stored in your private iCloud account and is not accessible to us.

Rouse Relay

If you choose to use the optional Rouse Relay feature for remote wake, wake requests are transmitted through a relay service. These requests contain only the information necessary to wake your target device (MAC address and broadcast address). No data is logged or retained by the relay after the request is processed.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not include third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at hello@oraserrata.net.
